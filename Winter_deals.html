Port Vista Oro
Your Hotel
Perfectly located bordering the Old Town, but still close to the livelier areas of Benidorm and the beaches, the Port Vista Oro has plenty to offer for both families and couples who want to get the best out of their holiday. A friendly personal service and refurbished public areas, inside and out, complete the package.
Location
300m to Levante and Poniente sandy beaches. Bars, restaurants and shops nearby. Main nightlife is 1.5km away.

Melas Lara Resort & Spa
Your Hotel
For a top-notch Turkish getaway, escape to the beachfront beauty that is the Melas Lara Resort & Spa. With exhilarating watersports, colourful slides and captivating sea views from its terraces, there's something here to keep even the pickiest of holidaymakers happy. Feast at the fish restaurant or get a taste of tradition with a modern twist at the Turkish à la carte. If you want to explore a little further, take a stroll to the boutiques and bars of classy Lara Beach.
Location
Situated directly on Lara beach. Shops, bars and restaurants only 500m away.
Facilities
Outdoor pool Watersports
Kids' pool Sauna
Sunloungers Playground
International buffet restaurant Pool bar
24hr reception Lounge
Wi-Fi (selected areas) Pool/beach towels
General Room Information
Rooms have a double or twin beds plus extra child bed, air conditioning, flat-screen television, tea and coffee making facilities, safe, hairdryer, bathrobes, bathroom or shower room and balcony.

Also available at a supplement: Side Sea View rooms. Family rooms (two-four people), with double bedroom, separate twin bedroom and side sea view. Twin rooms for sole use.
