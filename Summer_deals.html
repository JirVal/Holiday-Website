Liberty Hotel Lykia
Your Hotel
Just outside lively Kamari, this secluded hideaway offers the peace you need for a really relaxing beach holiday. You can unwind at the pool and soak up those lovely views of the sea, or take a stroll along the sand. And you'll still be in easy reach of all the resort's great restaurants and bars for a great evening out when you feel like it.
Location
Beside its own black-sand beach; 1km from the nearest restaurant; 3km from Kamari (hotel-run shuttle bus available)

Playa Olid & Siam Park
Your Hotel
When booking the stylish and modern Playa Olid, you'll also get unlimited access to Siam Park. It's voted the world's best waterpark, so hang on to your swimwear and take a deep breath before plunging 28m in a sheer drop down the Tower of Power, ride the largest artificial waves in the world at the Wave Palace, have a blast on the Volcano or simply relax on the Mai Thai river, the world's biggest lazy river.
Location
Situated 800m from the nearest beach and within 200m of a selection of bars and restaurants. 2.5km from the Playa de las Américas resort centre and Siam Park.
Facilities
Outdoor pool Watersports
Kids' pool Sauna
Sunloungers Playground
International buffet restaurant Pool bar
24hr reception Lounge

General Room Information
One bedroom apartments (up to three adults and one child) have a separate twin bedroom, living area with sofa bed, kitchenette (with fridge and cooking rings), tea and coffee making facilities, satellite television, hairdryer, full bathroom, and a balcony or terrace.

Also available at a supplement: Studios. Suites (up to four adults) have stylish décor and an open-plan design with twin beds, air conditioning, Wi-Fi and a whirlpool.
